Student Lessons Guide

Learning Assistance Lessons

Learning Assistance lessons are offered to children in Grade 1-3 who are experiencing difficulties with school work.

These children may attend individual lessons or group lessons with children who are facing similar problems (maximum of four children per group).

Remedial lessons are attended during the normal school day.

Extra Lessons

Extra Mathematics lessons are offered in the afternoons to children in Grades 4-7.

Tutoring may also be recommended in other subjects if a child requires some extra attention.

AI Tools & Assignments

Students are learning how to use AI tools for easier learning, faster writing, and timely completion of assignments and research.

Computer Lessons

All learners are introduced to computer lessons to build their digital skills from an early stage.
