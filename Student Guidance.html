Student Guidance

Every student is special! At our school, we support your dreams and help you grow with kindness, care, and confidence.

"Believe in yourself – you are braver than you think, smarter than you know, and stronger than you imagine!"

Whether you need help with schoolwork, want to talk to someone, or need encouragement, we're here for you. You are never alone!

Academic Support

Learning Support lessons are offered to children in Grade 1–3 who are experiencing difficulties with school work. These children may attend individual lessons or group lessons with children who are facing similar problems (maximum of four children per group). Remedial lessons are attended during the normal school day.

Extra Lessons
Extra Mathematics lessons are offered in the afternoons to children in Grades 4–7. Tutoring may also be recommended in other subjects if a child requires some extra attention.
