    Sporting Activities

    Physical exercise through participation in sports is essential for a balanced education. By learning and participating in sports, children not only satisfy their natural yearning for physical activity, they:

    • learn good sportsmanship, co-operation and team loyalty
    • improve co-ordination, balance and gross-motor skills
    • gain confidence, strengthen self-esteem and exercise self-control
    • experience less anxiety, more energy and better memory
    • demonstrate improved sustained attention in the classroom
    • have a better quality of sleep
    • strengthen friendships and increase overall physical fitness

    KaMhlushwa Primary School is fortunate to have superb on-site facilities along with a skilful, dedicated staff to provide instruction and guidance.
